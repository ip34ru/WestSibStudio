About Long

Long Enterprise is a small Russian company that makes very good equipment. For nearly thirty-year history, the company has gone from issuing built-up to the development and production of various processing devices and control of sound. Long Enterprise specialized in analog sound processing, and in this area the company has accumulated wide experience allows to produce high quality products that meet the modern global levels of development.
